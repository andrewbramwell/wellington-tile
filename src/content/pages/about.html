Who We are

Wellington's Tile and Grout Specialists

Based in Wellington, Wellington Tile Restorations started with a simple aim: to offer reliable tile maintenance and restoration in the region. Through time and consistent effort, we've expanded our services, handling tasks from re-grouting to tile cleaning.

Our strength lies in our years of hands-on experience in the tile industry, which informs our approach to every job. Serving Wellington and its nearby areas, we've built a reputation as dependable experts in tile and grout care. We take pride in the quality of our work and strive to provide good customer experiences. At Wellington Tile Restorations, each project reflects our commitment to our craft and the trust our clients place in us.
