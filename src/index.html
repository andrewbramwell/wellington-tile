Our Services

Residential & Commercial Tile & Grout Restoration

Wellington Tile Restoration brings a wide array of tile and grout solutions to both homes and businesses. Our unwavering commitment to top-notch quality, fair pricing, and guaranteed satisfaction defines every service we offer.

  • Tile Repair

    From minor chips to notable cracks, our proficient team stands ready to address any form of tile damage. We meticulously repair or replace any compromised tiles, ensuring your walls or flooring reflects their original splendor and charm.

  • Tile Regrouting

    Crumbling, stained, or cracked grout? We're on it. Depending on the degree of wear, we can touch-up grout, regrout entire surfaces or specific areas, or provide a perfect colour match to your existing grout for seamless aesthetics.

  • Grout Colouring (Colour Sealing, Staining)

    Transform the visual appeal of your grout with our color sealing technique. Beyond providing a uniform shade, this method also acts as a protective barrier. Ideal for instances of heavy staining, it's a practical alternative to a full re-grouting.

  • Tile & Grout Cleaning

    Rediscover the original brilliance of your tiles. With our professional cleaning methods, we efficiently eliminate dirt and stains, giving your floors a renewed shine and ensuring they remain protected against future marks.

Restoration

Tile Restoration in Wellington

Our tile restoration services provide proven solutions to restore and enhance all types of tiled flooring. Whether it's rectifying wear and tear or stabilising delicate mosaic patterns, we approach each task with utmost sensitivity. Our cleaning and sealing process revives your tiles, breathing new life into even the most aged designs.

At Wellington Tile Restoration, we're adept at handling both minor and extensive tile damage. Loose tiles are carefully lifted and reinstalled at the correct level, and then meticulously cleaned to remove ingrained dirt and residues. Our team can also replace damaged or missing tiles with new or salvaged ones that match your existing tiles. After a meticulous cleaning process, a carefully selected sealant is applied to lock in the rejuvenated appearance. Every restoration project we embark upon is aimed at revitalising your tiles, restoring their original charm and elegance.
